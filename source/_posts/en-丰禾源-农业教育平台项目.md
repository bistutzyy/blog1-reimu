## <span class="c-green">Background</span>

Traditional agricultural education cannot meet modern farmers' needs. FengHeyuan bridges this gap with integrated online-offline learning.

## <span class="c-red">Market Pain Points</span>

Information asymmetry is rampant. Farmers copy neighbors blindly — leading to losses. Post-harvest grain loss averages <span class="c-orange">4.09%</span>.

## <span class="c-blue">Our Solution</span>

**Online:** Personalized learning, video tutorials, dialect instruction for elderly farmers.
**Offline:** Field research, expert connections, community feedback.
