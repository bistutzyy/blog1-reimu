## The Poet of Hidden Wounds

Li Shangyin, courtesy name Yishan, with {% badge Du Mu blue %} formed "The Little Li-Du" — twin peaks of late Tang poetry. {% alertBlockquote success %}"Shangyin" sounds like "hidden wound" — fitting for his sorrowful life.{% endalertBlockquote %}

Father died at 9. Supported his mother by writing. Fortunate to meet mentor {% badge Linghu Chu blue %} and later marry {% badge Wang Yanmei red %}. But caught in the Niu-Li faction struggle — {% alertBlockquote danger %}Niu called him ungrateful. Li called him treacherous.{% endalertBlockquote %}

He simply wanted a lover, a warm home. And he achieved it: "One heart for a lifetime."

After his mother, mentor, and wife all passed — the ambitious youth became a lost man.

{% poem 夜雨寄北, Li Shangyin %}
君问归期未有期
  巴山夜雨涨秋池
何当共剪西窗烛
  却话巴山夜雨时
{% endpoem %}

*Written to his wife — who had already died.*

{% poem 锦瑟, Li Shangyin %}
锦瑟无端五十弦
  一弦一柱思华年
庄生晓梦迷蝴蝶
  望帝春心托杜鹃
沧海月明珠有泪
  蓝田日暖玉生烟
此情可待成追忆
  只是当时已惘然
{% endpoem %}
