## She Is Bilibili

**Bilibili is named after her.** <span class="c-blue">Misaka Mikoto</span>, ranked 3rd among Academy City's Level 5 espers — codename <span class="c-red">"The Railgun"</span>. Fans call her <span class="c-red">Biribiri</span>.

{% alertBlockquote tip SUCCESS %}
9-time champion of Kono Light Novel ga Sugoi! (2010-2020). 2011 International Saimoe winner. Over 55 tournament titles.
{% endalertBlockquote %}

## The Ace of Tokiwadai

{% badge Misaka Mikoto blue %}, 2nd year at Tokiwadai Middle School. "The Ace of Tokiwadai." Her skills: <span class="c-red">Railgun</span>, <span class="c-cyan">Lightning Spear</span>, <span class="c-purple">Iron Sand Sword</span>, <span class="c-green">Electromagnetic Hacking</span>.

{% alertBlockquote danger %}
**Level 6 Shift Project:** 20,000 clone "Sisters" sacrificed to evolve Accelerator. Mikoto stormed the lab alone, ready to die to end it. A 14-year-old girl carrying 20,000 lives.
{% endalertBlockquote %}
